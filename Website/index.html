    Our Cause

    High altitude balloons offer a low cost solution for deploying RF and sensor packages. These packages are designed for use in cases of natural disaster and other crisis. Traditionally, the flight time of an HAB is limited by the balloon bursting. The creation of an altitude control system will drastically improve the flight duration resulting in a larger variety of applications. Our proposed control system will allow for prolonged deployment over a specific region.
